The
Story

Established in 1991 with a vision to provide the best textile Products.

Over the years with an experience of over 31 years, Grover has diversified into products like hand-tufted carpets, woven carpets, woven rugs, woven cushions, throws, poufs, tufted bathmats, and machine-made carpets. We take tremendous pride in mentioning ourselves as the fastest-growing company in our industry. We managed to have a decent share in the market for our products in the USA, Europe, Middle East, and Australian markets. With the advantage of all the processes ranging from yarn procurement, dyeing, weaving, finishing, stitching, packing, and dispatch being in-house, we can pass on the benefit to our customers in the form of shorter lead times and cheaper products.
